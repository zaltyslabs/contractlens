Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

1. Our Commitment to Privacy

ContractLens takes your privacy seriously. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights. We do not sell your data. We do not retain your contracts.

2. Information We Collect

  • Account Information: Email address and authentication credentials (managed by Supabase Auth).
  • Contract Documents: Files you upload for analysis (PDF, DOCX, TXT). These are processed in memory and deleted immediately after analysis is complete.
  • Analysis Results: The AI-generated summaries and risk assessments are retained so you can access your report history.
  • Usage Data: Anonymous analytics (page views, feature usage) to improve the Service.
  • Payment Information: Processed by Stripe. We do not store your credit card details.

3. How We Process Your Contracts

When you upload a contract for analysis:

  1. The file is validated for safety (type, size, content).
  2. Text is extracted from the document.
  3. The extracted text is sanitized to remove any executable code or scripts.
  4. The text is sent to an AI language model for analysis.
  5. The analysis result is saved; the original file and extracted text are permanently deleted.

No human reads your contracts. Processing is fully automated.

4. Data Storage and Security

Account data and analysis results are stored in Supabase, which provides enterprise-grade security including encryption at rest and in transit. We implement file validation and sanitization to prevent malicious uploads. All uploaded files are sandboxed during processing and wiped after completion.

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Supabase — Authentication and database hosting.
  • Stripe — Payment processing.
  • AI Language Models — Contract analysis (text is sent to API; providers do not train on our data).

6. Your Rights

You have the right to: (a) access your personal data; (b) request deletion of your account and data; (c) export your analysis history; (d) withdraw consent at any time. To exercise these rights, contact us at the email below.

7. Data Retention

Analysis results are retained for the life of your account. Uploaded contract files are deleted immediately after processing. If you delete your account, all associated data is permanently removed within 30 days.

8. Cookies

We use essential cookies for authentication (via Supabase). We do not use tracking cookies or third-party analytics cookies.

9. Children's Privacy

The Service is not intended for users under 18. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. Material changes will be communicated via email. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance.

11. Contact

Privacy questions? Contact us at privacy@contractlens.io.
