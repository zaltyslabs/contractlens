What we check

Six danger zones.
Every contract. Every time.

We don't give you a generic score. Every zone gets a risk level, the exact clause that triggered it, and a concrete recommendation.

Payment Terms
When do you actually get paid?
When and how you get paid, late payment fees, holdback provisions, and clawback clauses that let clients demand money back.
Net-30/60/90 Late fees Clawbacks Milestones
IP & Ownership
Who owns your work?
Work-for-hire clauses, who holds copyright after delivery, portfolio and case-study rights, and future-use restrictions.
Work-for-hire Portfolio rights Copyright Moral rights
Non-Compete
Can you work for their competitors?
Scope, duration, and geographic limits of non-compete clauses. Broad "similar work" definitions that can shut you out of an industry.
Duration Geographic scope Industry limits Non-solicit
Termination
How do you get out?
Exit clauses, notice periods required by each party, auto-renewal traps, and what happens to work-in-progress on termination.
Notice periods Auto-renewal Kill fees Termination for cause
Indemnification
Who pays if things go wrong?
Who is liable for what, whether indemnification is mutual or one-sided, and whether you're covering the client's legal defense costs.
Mutual vs one-sided Third-party claims Defense costs
Liability Caps
How much can they sue you for?
Maximum damage limits, exclusions for IP infringement or willful misconduct, and uncapped liability buried in carve-outs.
Damage caps Consequential damages Carve-outs
