What We Check

Six danger zones. Every contract. Every time.

💰 Payment Terms

When and how you get paid. Net-30? Late fees? Clawbacks?

🔒 IP & Ownership

Who owns your work? Can you use it in your portfolio?

🚫 Non-Compete

Can you work for competitors? For how long?

⏰ Termination

How to get out. Notice periods. Auto-renewal traps.

🛡️ Indemnification

Who pays if things go wrong? Is it mutual?

⚖️ Liability Caps

How much can they sue you for? Are damages capped?
